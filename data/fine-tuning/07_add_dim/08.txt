what is the total revenue for premium users with auto renewal?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: subscriptions
    columns:
      - name: auto_renewal
        description: Indicates if the subscription has auto renewal.
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: boolean
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - total_revenue
- filters:
    - subscription.plan = "premium"
    - auto_renewal: true
