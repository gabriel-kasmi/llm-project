give me the number of users who made standard subscriptions in january 2020
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: subscriptions
    columns:
      - name: start_date
        description: The start date of the subscription. 
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: date
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - number_of_unique_users
- filters:
    - start_date[MONTH] = 1
    - start_date[YEAR] = 2020
    - subscription.plan = standard
