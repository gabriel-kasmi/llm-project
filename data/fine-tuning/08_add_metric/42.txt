what is the number of cancelled orders by weekday/weekend ?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    columns:
      - name: order_id
        description: Unique ID for orders.
        meta: 
          metrics:
            number_of_cancelled_orders: 
              description: Number of cancelled orders.
              type: count
              filters:
                - order_status: cancelled
- dimensions:
    - is_weekday_or_weekend
- metrics:
    - number_of_cancelled_orders
- filters: []
