what is the US vs non-US revenue contribution by provider ?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      metrics:
        non_us_revenue_percentage:
          description: Percent of total global revenue coming from outside the US.
          type: number
          sql: 1 - ${us_revenue_percentage}
          format: 0.00%
- dimensions:
    - provider_name
- metrics:
    - us_revenue_percentage
    - non_us_revenue_percentage
- filters: []
