what is the average processing duration of orders placed on weekdays?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta: 
      metrics:
        processing_duration_days:
          description: Processing duration of the order
          type: number
          sql: DATEDIFF(MONTH, ${TABLE}.order_date, ${TABLE}.order_fulfilled_at)
        avg_processing_duration_days:
          description: Average processing duration of orders
          type: number
          sql: avg(${processing_duration_days})
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - avg_processing_duration_days
- filters:
    - is_weekday_or_weekend = "weekday"
