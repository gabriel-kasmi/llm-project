give me the average duration of subscriptions of premium users
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: subscriptions
    meta:
      metrics:
        subscription_duration_months:
          description: Subscription duration
          type: number
          sql: DATEDIFF(MONTH, ${TABLE}.start_date, ${TABLE}.end_date)
        average_subscription_duration_months:
          description: Average subscription duration
          type: number
          sql: avg(${subscription_duration_months})
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - average_subscription_duration_months
- filters: 
    - is_premium: true
