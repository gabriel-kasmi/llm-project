what is the total refund amount in january 2024?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    columns:
      - name: refund_amount
        description: The amount refunded for the order.
        meta:
          metrics: 
            total_refund_amount: 
              description: Total refund amount.
              type: sum
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - total_refund_amount
- filters:
    - order_date[YEAR] = 2024
    - order_date[MONTH] = 1
