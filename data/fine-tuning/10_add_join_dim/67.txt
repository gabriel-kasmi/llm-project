show me the list of products with reviews in April 2020?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: reviews 
          sql_on: ${orders.review_id} = ${reviews.review_id}
  - name: reviews
    meta:
      label: Reviews
    columns:
      - name: review_id
        description: The unique ID for reviews
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: review_date
        description: The date of review
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: date
            time_intervals:
              - DAY
              - MONTH
              - QUARTER
              - YEAR
- dimensions:
    - product_name
- metrics: []
- filters: 
    - review_date[YEAR] = 2020
    - review_date[MONTH] = 4
