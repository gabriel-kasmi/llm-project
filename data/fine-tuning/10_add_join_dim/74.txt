what are the types of promotions that were assocaited with orders in 2020?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: promotions 
          sql_on: ${orders.product_id} = ${promotions.product_id}
  - name: promotions
    meta:
      label: Promotions
    columns:
      - name: promotion_id
        description: The unique ID of promotion
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: promotion_type
        description: Promotion type
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
- dimensions:
    - promotion_type
- metrics: []
- filters: 
    - order_date[YEAR] = 2020
