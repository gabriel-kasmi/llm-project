what is the total amount of payments per payment method in 2020?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: payments 
          sql_on: ${orders.payment_id} = ${payments.payment_id}
  - name: payments
    meta:
      label: Payments
    columns:
      - name: payment_id
        description: The unique ID of payment
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: payment_amount
        description: amount of payments received
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: number
          metrics: 
            total_payment_amount:
              description: Total amount of payments received
              type: sum
      - name: payment_method
        description: Payment method
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: payment_date
        description: The date of payment
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: date
            intervals:
              - DAY
              - MONTH
              - YEAR
- dimensions:
    - payment_method
- metrics:
    - total_payment_amount
- filters: 
    - payment_date[YEAR] = 2020
