what is the number of reviews in decembre 2023?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: reviews 
          sql_on: ${orders.review_id} = ${reviews.review_id}
  - name: reviews
    meta:
      label: Reviews
    columns:
      - name: review_id
        description: The unique ID for reviews
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
          metrics: 
            number_of_reviews:
              description: Average number of reviews
              type: count
      - name: review_date
        description: Review date
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: date
            time_intervals:
              - DAY
              - MONTH
              - QUARTER
              - YEAR
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - number_of_reviews
- filters: 
    - review_date[YEAR] = 2023
    - review_date[MONTH] = 12
