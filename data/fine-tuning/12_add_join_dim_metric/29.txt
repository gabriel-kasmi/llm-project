what is the total number of payments per payment method?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: payments 
          sql_on: ${orders.payment_id} = ${payments.payment_id}
  - name: payments
    meta:
      label: Payments
    columns:
      - name: payment_id
        description: The unique ID of payment
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
          metrics: 
            number_of_payments:
              description: Total number of payments received.
              type: count
      - name: payment_method
        description: Payment method
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
- dimensions:
    - payment_method
- metrics:
    - number_of_payments
- filters: []
