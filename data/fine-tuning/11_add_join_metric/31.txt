what is the average of review ratings per product?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: reviews 
          sql_on: ${orders.review_id} = ${reviews.review_id}
  - name: reviews
    meta:
      label: Reviews
    columns:
      - name: review_id
        description: The unique ID for reviews
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: review_rating
        description: order review rating
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: number
          metrics: 
            avg_of_review_ratings:
              description: Average number of reviews
              type: average
- dimensions:
    - product_name
- metrics:
    - avg_of_review_ratings
- filters: []
