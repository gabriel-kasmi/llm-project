give me the average duration of promotions on orders in the UK?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: promotions 
          sql_on: ${orders.product_id} = ${promotions.product_id}
  - name: promotions
    meta:
      label: Promotions
    metrics:
      discount_duration_days: 
        description: Average time taken to fulfill an order (in days).
        type: number
        sql: DATEDIFF(DAY, ${TABLE}.start_date, ${TABLE}.end_date)
      avg_discount_duration_days: 
        description: Average time taken to fulfill an order (in days).
        type: number
        sql: avg(${discount_duration_days})
    columns:
      - name: promotion_id
        description: The unique ID of promotion
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - avg_discount_duration_days
- filters:
    - order_country = UK
