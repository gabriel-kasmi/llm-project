give me the average of rating with the total revenu of orders by month in 2020
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: reviews 
          sql_on: ${orders.review_id} = ${reviews.review_id}
  - name: reviews
    meta:
      label: Reviews
    columns:
      - name: review_id
        description: The unique ID for reviews
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: review_rating
        description: order review rating
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: number
          metrics: 
            avg_of_review_ratings:
              description: Average number of reviews
              type: average
- dimensions:
    - order_date[MONTH]
- metrics:
    - total_revenue
    - avg_of_review_ratings
- filters:
    - order_date[YEAR] = 2020
