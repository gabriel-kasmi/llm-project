what is the total amount of payments received?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    meta:
      joins:
        - join: payments 
          sql_on: ${orders.payment_id} = ${payments.payment_id}
  - name: payments
    meta:
      label: Payments
    columns:
      - name: payment_id
        description: Unique ID for payments
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: string
      - name: payment_amount
        description: amount of payments received
        meta:
          dimension:
            type: number
          metrics: 
            total_payment_amount:
              description: Total amount of payments received
              type: sum
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - total_payment_amount
- filters: []
