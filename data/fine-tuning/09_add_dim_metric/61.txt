what is the total refund amount for orders that were fullfilled in 2022?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    columns:
      - name: refund_amount
        description: The amount refunded for the order.
        meta:
          metrics: 
            total_refund_amount: 
              description: Total refund amount.
              type: sum
      - name: order_fulfilled_at 
        description: the date on which the order was officially fullfilled
        meta: 
          dimension:
            type: date
- dimensions: []
- metrics:
    - total_refund_amount
- filters:
    - order_fulfilled_at[YEAR] = 2022
