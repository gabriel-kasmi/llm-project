what is the number of orders with vouchers grouped by voucher partner name?
=====
- dimensions and metrics to add:
  - name: orders
    columns:
      - name: voucher_partner_name
        description: Voucher partner name
        meta: 
          dimension: 
            type: boolean
      - name: voucher_discount_amount
        description: Voucher discount amount
        meta:
          metrics:
            number_of_orders_with_vouchers:
              description: Number of orders with vouchers
              type: count
              filters:
                - voucher_discount_amount > 0
- dimensions:
    - voucher_partner_name
- metrics:
    - number_of_orders_with_vouchers
- filters: []
